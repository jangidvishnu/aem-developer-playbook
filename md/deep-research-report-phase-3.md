# Top AEM Employers Worldwide

Based on public case studies and usage data, the most attractive employers for a senior AEM (Adobe Experience Manager) engineer include a mix of product companies and large enterprises that have invested in Adobe’s digital experience platform. The table below highlights key examples (with ranked priority for an AEM engineer) along with evidence of AEM usage, India presence, and other factors:

- **Adobe Inc.** – *Product / Software (HQ: San Jose, USA)* – Adobe is the creator of AEM, and of course uses AEM internally and with its clients.  India presence: **Yes** (R&D offices in Bangalore, Noida).  Careers: [Adobe Careers](https://careers.adobe.com)​.  **Score: 10/10** – As the product owner, Adobe offers the strongest AEM skill match, top pay, great tech culture, and relocation opportunities (internal transfers, visa support).

- **Mastercard** – *Product (FinServ, HQ: New York, USA)* – Global payment brand that publicly credits AEM in its marketing platform. India presence: **Yes** (large tech centers in Bangalore/Gurgaon).  Careers: [Mastercard Jobs](https://mastercard.taleo.net)​.  **Score: 9.5** – Strong engineering culture, attractive fintech employer, visa sponsorship (global mobility), and modern tech stack.

- **Cisco Systems** – *Product (Networking, HQ: San Jose, USA)* – Uses AEM to “deliver personalized experiences at scale” with Adobe. India presence: **Yes** (major campuses in Bangalore, etc.).  Careers: [Cisco Careers](https://jobs.cisco.com)​.  **Score: 9.4** – Top-tier tech company, excellent engineering culture, well-known for R&D. Supports international transfers and hires aggressively in India.

- **Philips** – *Product (HealthTech/Electronics, HQ: Amsterdam, NL)* – Electronics/healthcare leader that “built the backbone of its new digital marketing platform” on AEM. India presence: **Yes** (R&D centers in Bangalore, Noida, Chennai).  Careers: [Philips Careers](https://www.careers.philips.com)​.  **Score: 9.3** – Globally respected brand, innovative projects in health tech, good work-life balance, offers relocation to Netherlands.

- **JPMorgan Chase** – *GCC/FinServ (HQ: New York, USA)* – Leading bank whose Asset & Wealth Management unit consolidated its CMS onto AEM. India presence: **Yes** (large tech hubs in Mumbai, Bangalore).  Careers: [JPMorgan Careers](https://careers.jpmorgan.com)​.  **Score: 9.2** – Excellent pay and training, technology-driven finance, strong global mobility.

- **Walmart (Walmart Global Tech)** – *GCC/Retail (HQ: Bentonville, USA)* – The retail giant built one of the “world’s largest intranets” on Adobe Experience Manager. India presence: **Yes** (Walmart Global Tech offices in India).  Careers: [Walmart Careers](https://careers.walmart.com)​.  **Score: 9.0** – Massive tech organization, good compensation, strong internal career development.

- **Verizon** – *Enterprise (Telecom, HQ: New York, USA)* – Uses AEM in its Adobe-powered marketing platform (dynamic media, video). India presence: **No** (mostly US).  Careers: [Verizon Careers](https://careers.verizon.com)​.  **Score: 8.5** – Top telecom firm, high pay, advanced network technology, but limited India hiring.

- **Under Armour** – *Product (Retail/Apparel, HQ: Baltimore, USA)* – Sportswear brand cited as an AEM user in Adobe’s announcement. India presence: **No**.  Careers: [Under Armour Jobs](https://careers.underarmour.com)​.  **Score: 7.5** – Fashion tech environment, good web/CMS work, moderate pay, US-focused.

- **MGM Resorts** – *Enterprise (Hospitality, HQ: Las Vegas, USA)* – Casino/hotel operator leveraging AEM for personalized customer experiences. India presence: **No**.  Careers: [MGM Careers](https://jobs.mgmresorts.com)​.  **Score: 7.0** – Cutting-edge hospitality tech projects, solid comp, but no India offices.

This list is illustrative of the **highest-priority companies**. In addition, many other global firms use AEM internally even if they don’t advertise it explicitly. For example, **Accenture/Deloitte Digital** (Adobe Partners), **Microsoft**, **ServiceNow**, **SAP**, **Bank of America**, **Disney**, **BMW**, **Bosch**, **Siemens**, **Delta Air Lines**, **Airlines (Emirates, Lufthansa, United)**, **Pharmaceuticals (Roche, Medtronic, GE Healthcare)**, **Retailers (Target, IKEA, Best Buy)** and **government contractors** all have active AEM initiatives (often visible via case studies or jobs titled “Digital Experience Engineer”). These firms typically offer strong engineering culture, global mobility programs, and competitive salaries. 

**Search Tips:** Don’t limit yourself to “AEM Developer” – look for roles like *Web Platform Engineer*, *Digital Experience Engineer*, *CMS Engineer*, or just *Software Engineer* at these companies. Use site-specific searches or the company’s career page (links above) and keywords like “Adobe Experience Manager” or “AEM Sites” to uncover hidden opportunities.

**Sources:** Verified from Adobe case studies, press releases, and customer stories, as well as industry technology reports on companies using AEM. These sources confirm each listed company’s use of AEM and help assess engineering culture, global footprint, and visa support.

# Executive Summary  

Major global enterprises across industries have adopted Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) for their digital platforms. For example, Philips unified global content delivery using AEM managed services, and Cisco explicitly advertises AEM cloud developer roles.  American Express’ own website documentation confirms AEM as its CMS, and surveys of web technologies list companies like Microsoft, Walmart, Bank of America and India’s ICICI Bank among AEM users.  The CSV below catalogs ~100 such Global Capability Center (GCC) companies, detailing industry, HQ location, India presence, hiring evidence, careers pages, AEM usage evidence, typical AEM-related job titles (with sample postings), visa sponsorship info, and notes (e.g. migrations or Adobe references).
